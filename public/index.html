    About mirageJS
    
              
    MirageJS is a next-generation video-chat platform built on webRTC
                technology where users can interact with both 2d and 3d objects
                in a augmented reality based interface.
    
              
    Integrating applied physics and movement tracking functionalities,
                this is the video chat app to use in the 21st century
